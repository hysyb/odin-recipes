Eggs Benedict

Description

Starting with a freshly baked english muffin topped with black forest ham, a perfectly poached egg, and creamy holindaise sauce

  • English Muffin
  • Black Forest Ham
  • Egg
  • Holindaise Sauce
  1. Soft poach an egg as you usually would
  2. Prepare holindaise sauce
  3. Toast english muffin
  4. Fry ham in fryin pan
  5. Assemble the eggs benedict and drizzle holindaise sauce over top
